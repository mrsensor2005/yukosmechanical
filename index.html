Frequently Asked Questions



YUKOS Mechanical is your specialist to upgrade your heating and cooling system. During your Free In-Home Consultation we can advise you on which heating and cooling system is best for you and your family. A unit that is too large or too small will often increase heating and cooling costs as well as cause high variations in temperature and humidity. Your consultation can also include an assessment of your ductwork. Ductwork is very important when a system is replaced; without proper sealing and the correct duct size, your home can lose all the efficiency gained by the new system.


What is the average life expectancy of a system?

Most systems have a lifetime of 10 to 20 years. As your equipment gets older, its efficiency can decrease dramatically. A free in-home consultation can determine if you require HVAC system needs.

What determines if I need to replace rather than repair my unit?

If your unit is 7 years old or older, requires frequent repairs or stops working, fails to heat or cool areas of your home, runs excessively or constantly turns on and off, or produces high utility bills, it can be more economical to schedule a free in-home consultation and replace your existing system.

Will I need a permit?

Permit requirements vary by location. Some municipalities do require a permit. If it is required, we will obtain a permit during your scheduled HVAC service.
